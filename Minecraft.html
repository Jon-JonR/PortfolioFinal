Minecraft Art

Minecraft is a video game that is used for both fun and for creative use. However, the creative side

of Minecraft includes various ways of building up 3d models using blocks from the bottom to the top.

With this knowledge, I decided to create a 3d model of an anime figure specifically a character from

the series, "Bocchi the Rock" a reference was used to create the model, however, the images shown are

completely built by me with my own style.
