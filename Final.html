Digital Media Final

For the 2023 Intro to Digital Media Final. I continued to research in the media

art of the Meme Mashup project I recently worked on. For this project, I thought of

the idea of adding anime characters into real life scenarios and photographs. Now how

does this affect the media side? The reason is to convey the certain desire of most

anime fans of wanting to be able to see an anime character in real life as depicted

in each image. Despite not having an actual story to go by, the story that can be depicted

from the collection of photographs is rather a collection of how it would look like if this

absurd idea were to actually come alive. With that in mind, the programs used to create

this include adobe photoshop and capcut. The music used is created by Snail house and

is named "Hot Milk" The video was originally inspired by youtuber iGeeks and is rather

a remake of the video with my own images.
