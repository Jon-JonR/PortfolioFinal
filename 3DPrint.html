3D Print Art

Deriving from the Minecraft Art Project, the 3D Print project consisted of grabbing the certain model

from Minecraft and processing it into a separate program turning the model into a FPP and a STL file.

The model is then 3d printed using a 3d printer provided by the school. Afterwards, I used acryllic paint

to accurately paint the model to match the ingame coloring rather than having a pale white color.
