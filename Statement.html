Jon-Jon Rodriguez's About

Beginning during my freshmen year in High School, I had the intention of enrolling

in an intro to animation class. I took the class as a way to improve my own skills as an

artist and to specifically improve my drawing skills. During my sophomore year I had a break

from art-wise courses to focus more on general education taking 2 art courses during my Senior

Year. My junior year of high school is when I started really become invested into the idea of

creating art for a living. With many experience of playing video games and drawing, I figured

that I could combine the two to create a vision for the future.

____

Currently as a freshmen in San Jose State University, I continue to improve my skills as a growing

artist. Despite not getting into the Animation & Illustration program, I've decided to get the Digital

Media Major due to the research of creating Computer Games. In the future as an artist, I hope to be

able to be involved in the development of certain video games as an artist and to create artwork that

could not only inspire me to make more, but to inspire others to create. I wish to create art as both

as a hobby, and as a living.

_____
