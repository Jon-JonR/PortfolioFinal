Meme Mashup

The Meme Mashup Art consisted of creating a unique kind of image that combined various unlikely things

to create a whole new scene. They are often used for humor as a way to compare two unlikely things as a new

mutated version. For the Meme Mashup shown in this portfolio, I decided to choose a familiar meme and to

mutate it into a rather humorous way. The references that the meme represented including the manga series,

"Jojo's Bizarre Adventures" and the 2023 movie "Barbie." Many other references were shown in the mashup all

to unite them into a humorous scene that is meant to bring a laugh.
