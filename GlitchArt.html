Glitch Art

Glitch Art consists of the transformation of normal image files into raw data and tinkering with the

text in order to affect the image itself when returned back into an image file. The images shown went

through a change whether it was changed through text, or was modified through the use of audio editing.

When creating these Glitch Art I had a specific theme in mind when adding them to my Portfolio. The first

two images were chosen from personal preference as a way to explore the beginner use of glitch art and how

to use the program. The last three images had a similarity; being part of the same tv series that I personally

grew up with. I figured that creating with this in mind could really bring out the intention of the art.
